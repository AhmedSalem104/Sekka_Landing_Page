## 🎯 SHORT VERSION (Copy-paste ready — 1 prompt)

> **Use this for:** Sora, Kling, Runway, Pika — single-shot or short clip

```
A cinematic 30-second promo video for "Sekka" — a smart delivery management app for motorcycle riders in Cairo, Egypt.

Open: young Egyptian delivery driver on an orange Vespa-style scooter navigating busy Cairo streets at golden hour, stressed by WhatsApp notifications on his phone. Quick cut to a glowing orange-and-white phone screen showing the Sekka app: a clean Arabic-language dashboard with order cards, a map with optimized route pins connecting in real-time, and live earnings counter climbing. Split shots show: (1) pasting a WhatsApp message into the app that instantly converts into a structured order, (2) the driver speaking into his phone while riding and text appearing, (3) a smooth route drawing itself across a Cairo map with the scooter following the line. Final scene: the same driver now smiling, riding confidently at sunset past a modern Cairo skyline. End card: the Sekka logo (an orange scooter on a curved road with Arabic letters) with Google Play and App Store badges.

Style: modern cinematic, warm golden-hour lighting, dynamic gimbal camera, fast-paced edits synced to an upbeat Middle Eastern electronic fusion track at 120 BPM. Color palette: vibrant orange (#FC5D01), deep navy (#2D3748), clean white.

Arabic text overlays throughout (Tajawal font, bold):
- "طلبات كتير ووقت ضيّق؟"
- "سِكّة — شريك شغلك في الديليفري"
- "الصق من الواتساب"
- "مسار ذكي بالـ AI"
- "أرباحك لحظة بلحظة"
- "حمّل التطبيق دلوقتي"

Aspect ratio: 9:16 vertical for social media (or 16:9 horizontal for YouTube/web).
```

### 🎬 SCENE 1 — "The Hook" (0–4s)

**Visual:**
Young Egyptian man (25–35) on an orange Vespa-style delivery scooter, stopped at a red traffic light in busy Cairo morning traffic. His phone buzzes repeatedly with WhatsApp notifications. He looks confused and overwhelmed, eyes darting between screen and road.

**Camera:** Slow push-in on his face, motion blur around him
**Sound:** Urban ambient (traffic, horns), multiple WhatsApp notification beeps
**Text overlay:** `طلبات كتير... ووقت ضيّق؟` *(Too many orders... tight on time?)*

---

### 🎬 SCENE 2 — "The Struggle" (4–8s)

**Visual:**
Close-up of his hands trying to scribble addresses on a crumpled piece of paper while checking his phone. Time-lapse of a clock speeding past. His mood darkens. A map on his phone shows a zigzag inefficient route.

**Camera:** Overhead close-up of hands, cut to POV driving shot
**Text overlay:** `وإنت معرفتش خلصت كام النهاردة؟` *(And you don't know how many you delivered today?)*

---

### 🎬 SCENE 3 — "Enter Sekka" (8–14s)

**Visual:**
Transition: his phone screen dims, then lights up with a warm orange glow. The Sekka logo appears — an orange scooter riding on a curved dark-navy road with Arabic calligraphy "سِكّة". Logo animates in with a satisfying snap.

Then the app UI reveals itself: a clean dashboard with order cards, order count, earnings, and a map.

**Camera:** Match cut from the stressed face to the bright app screen
**Sound:** Soft chime, music beat drops
**Text overlay:** `اتعرّف على سِكّة` *(Meet Sekka)*
**Tagline overlay:** `شريك شغلك في الديليفري` *(Your delivery work partner)*

---

### 🎬 SCENE 4 — "Paste from WhatsApp" (14–22s)

**Visual:**
Split screen. Left side: his thumb copying a WhatsApp message ("أحمد محمد - 15 شارع النصر المعادي - 01012345678 - 200 ج"). Right side: the Sekka app, an order card materializing with all fields auto-filled (name, address, phone, amount).

An animated checkmark appears with a subtle whoosh.

**Camera:** Clean UI close-up, subtle zoom on the auto-fill moment
**Text overlay:** `الصق من الواتساب — والتطبيق يقراها لوحده` *(Paste from WhatsApp — the app reads it for you)*

---

### 🎬 SCENE 5 — "Smart Routing" (22–30s)

**Visual:**
Top-down map view of Cairo with 10 scattered order pins. An AI-style animation sweeps across the map. The pins reorganize themselves into an optimal sequence. A glowing orange line draws itself connecting them in the smartest path.

Comparison overlay:
- Before: `95 كم / 4 ساعات` *(95 km / 4 hours)*
- After: `48 كم / 2 ساعات` *(48 km / 2 hours)*

A tiny animated scooter follows the new route.

**Camera:** Top-down map, zoom-out to see the full optimized journey
**Text overlay:** `مسار ذكي بالـ AI — وفّر نص المسافة` *(Smart AI routing — save half the distance)*

---

### 🎬 SCENE 6 — "Voice Input" (30–38s)

**Visual:**
Close-up of the driver's mouth as he rides. A voice waveform visualization pulses around him. As he speaks, Arabic text appears on the app: `"أضف طلب لسارة علي، شارع التحرير، 150 جنيه كاش"`.

The app instantly creates the order. A confirmation checkmark.

**Camera:** Tight face close-up, follow shot of scooter in motion
**Sound:** His voice (subtle), voice-recognition tones
**Text overlay:** `كلّمه وأنت سايق — وهو يسجّل لك` *(Talk while riding — and it logs for you)*

---

### 🎬 SCENE 7 — "Live Earnings" (38–46s)

**Visual:**
Dashboard animation: a large circular progress ring fills up to 85%. Numbers count up live:
- Orders: `12 → 18 → 20`
- Earnings: `120 → 350 → 450 ج`
- Distance saved: `47 كم`

Green checkmarks appear. A small celebratory burst of orange confetti.

**Camera:** UI close-up with smooth animations
**Text overlay:** `أرباحك واضحة لحظة بلحظة` *(Your earnings clear, live every second)*

---

### 🎬 SCENE 8 — "The Transformation" (46–54s)

**Visual:**
The same driver from Scene 1 — but now he's smiling, confident, riding smoothly through Cairo at golden hour. Cars pass him cleanly, sunlight rim-lights his helmet, the city rolls by behind him.

He glances at his phone mounted on the scooter — the Sekka dashboard is showing success stats.

**Camera:** Tracking shot following the scooter, sunset backdrop
**Text overlay:** `من 8 طلبات لـ 18 طلب — بدون تعب` *(From 8 orders to 18 — without the exhaustion)*

---

### 🎬 SCENE 9 — "Call to Action" (54–60s)

**Visual:**
Hero shot of the Sekka logo (orange scooter on navy road) growing in the center. Behind it, a soft orange gradient pulses.

Google Play and App Store badges slide in from the bottom. The app icon animates with a subtle bounce.

**Camera:** Center-framed logo, subtle zoom
**Sound:** Musical sting, uplifting
**Text overlay (big):** `سِكّة — شريك شغلك في الديليفري` *(Sekka — Your delivery work partner)*
**Text overlay (subtle):** `حمّل التطبيق دلوقتي` *(Download now)*
